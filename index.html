Maicon Rodrigues Albuquerque

Undergraduate degree in Physical Education (2007) from Centro Universitário de Belo Horizonte (UNIBH). Master’s in Sports Sciences (2010) and Ph.D. in Molecular Medicine (2013), both from the Universidade Federal de Minas Gerais (UFMG). Currently, I am an Full Professor in the Department of Sports at UFMG and act as the Director of UFMG’s Sports Training Center. I have also been the coordinator of the Graduate Program in Sports Sciences (PPGCE) at UFMG from 2018 to 2020, and the deputy coordinator of the same program in 2021. Additionally, I am a permanent faculty member at both the master’s and Ph.D. levels in the PPGCE as well as in the Graduate Program in Psychology: Cognition and Behavior (PPGCOGCOM). I have been a board member of the Brazilian Society of Neuropsychology from 2019 to the present. My areas of expertise include Sports and Neuropsychology, with an emphasis on Neuroscience/Sports Psychology, Combat Sports, and Genetics.
